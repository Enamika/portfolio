CHOUFOUNIIIII !!
Hi, I’m Imane Charki, but most people know me as Eni — a Moroccan photographer with a curious eye and a heart drawn to stories that live in the quiet and the ordinary.
I believe photography isn’t just about taking pictures — it’s about listening. Every image I make is a response to something that spoke to me, whether it’s a fleeting shadow, an aging wall, or the silent poetry in a person’s gesture. My lens is drawn to everyday beauty, subtle details, and the emotional threads that connect us to places and each other. Currently based in Casablanca and studying at ENSAD, I explore photography as both an artistic practice and a means of cultural expression. My work often drifts between documentary and poetic narrative, with a strong connection to identity, memory, and space. I’m always seeking out collaboration, learning, and new ways to tell visual stories — whether through digital series, printed books, or multidisciplinary projects. If you're curious, inspired, or just want to talk art over coffee (real or virtual), I’d love to connect. Let’s make something meaningful together.

LOOK WHAT I CAN DO!
01
PHOTO SHOOTING
Whether you’re looking to capture the magic of a special moment, elevate your personal brand, or tell a visual story that feels true to you — I offer personalized photoshooting services that blend authenticity, aesthetics, and emotion. From portraits and creative editorials to event coverage and artistic collaborations, each session is crafted with care, using both natural and styled approaches. I guide you gently throughout the shoot so you feel comfortable, seen, and radiant in every frame. Let’s co-create timeless visuals that speak from the heart.
02
PHOTO EDITING
Behind every powerful image is a touch of magic — and that’s where my photo editing service comes in. I carefully enhance each photograph to bring out its full emotional and visual potential, while always respecting the soul of the shot. Whether it’s retouching portraits, correcting colors, creating dreamy atmospheres, or giving your visuals a bold, artistic edge, I use professional tools and a trained eye to make every detail shine. My goal? To elevate your photos into expressive, polished works of art that still feel natural and uniquely you.
03
GRAPHIC DESIGN
From posters and flyers to social media visuals and portfolio layouts, I create bold, clean, and emotionally resonant graphic designs that speak your language. My work blends modern aesthetics with cultural nuances, always tailored to your story and purpose. Whether you're an artist, brand, or student, I’ll help you turn your vision into compelling visual pieces — print or digital — that truly stand out.
04
PHOTO RESTORING
Old photos hold stories that deserve to be remembered. I offer photo restoration services that carefully bring damaged, faded, or aged images back to life — with sensitivity and respect for their original charm. Using digital tools and an artistic eye, I remove cracks, enhance details, and recover tones to preserve memories for future generations. It’s not just editing — it’s reviving family history and emotional heritage.
